Travel to Hawaii

Why you should travel to Hawaii:

  1. A fascinating history
  2. Incredible nature & wildlife
  3. A mix of City & Serenity

Hawaii is a U.S. state located in the central Pacific Ocean, consisting of 137 islands, with eight main ones, including Oahu, Maui, and the Big Island. Its capital is Honolulu. Known for its volcanic landscapes, tropical climate, and rich Polynesian culture, Hawaii is a popular tourist destination. The state is home to active volcanoes like Mauna Loa and Kilauea. It has a diverse ecosystem with unique wildlife and is famous for surfing, hula, and traditional Hawaiian cuisine. Hawaii became the 50th U.S. state in 1959.

Top things to do in Hawaii:

  • Visit Waikiki Beach – Famous for surfing and stunning sunsets
  • Explore Pearl Harbor & USS Arizona Memorial – Historic WWII site
  • Hike Diamond Head Crater – Offers breathtaking panoramic views
  • Experience Polynesian Cultural Center – Learn about Hawaiian culture

Hawaii is the most isolated island chain in the world, located over 2,400 miles from the nearest continent. It’s home to Mauna Kea, the tallest mountain on Earth when measured from its base on the ocean floor. Hawaii also has the only royal palace in the U.S., Iolani Palace, and is the birthplace of surfing. The islands grow unique plants like the silversword, which blooms once in its lifetime, and the state has its own time zone, Hawaiian Standard Time (HST), with no daylight saving time.
