Recipe #1: Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Ground beef 500g
Tomato sauce 400g
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: Boil the spaghetti according to the package instructions.

Step 2: In a separate pan, saut√© garlic in olive oil, then add the ground beef and cook until browned.

Step 3: Add the tomato sauce to the beef and simmer for 20 minutes.

Step 4: Combine the sauce with the spaghetti and serve hot.
